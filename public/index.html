How It Works

1. Snap a photo of a card or binder page
2. AI identifies the exact card, set, number & condition
3. Prices pulled from Cardmarket (English, lowest) & eBay sold
4. Buy price calculated at your chosen percentage

Accuracy tips: Good lighting, capture the full card including edges for condition grading. For binder pages, hold the phone level and get all 9 pockets in frame.
